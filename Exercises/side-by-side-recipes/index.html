Greek Chicken and Potato Sheet Pan Bake

Prep Time: 15 Minutes Cook Time: 50-55 Minutes

Total Time: 1 Hour 10 Minutes Yields: 4-6

This Greek chicken and potato bake proves you don’t need a long ingredient list to get a great result. Everything roasts together on one pan, which means the chicken flavors the potatoes and the potatoes crisp up right next to the chicken. It’s straightforward, reliable, and perfect for a weeknight.
